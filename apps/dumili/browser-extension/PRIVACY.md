# Privacy Policy - Dumili Auto-indexer

**Last updated:** March 1, 2025

## Overview

Dumili Auto-indexer is a Chrome/Firefox extension that helps you fill the Inducks online indexing form using Dumili text output.

## Data Collection and Use

- **Local storage only:** Text you paste into the extension is stored temporarily in your browser's session storage. It is never sent to our servers.

- **No external transmission:** We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

- **Form submissions:** When you submit the Inducks form, data goes directly to inducks.org in accordance with their privacy practices. We do not intercept or store that data.

## Permissions

- **Content script on inducks.org:** The extension runs solely on inducks.org/onlineinx.php to provide the helper interface. No additional permissions are required.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

## Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in the project repository.
